Movie summary

Baahubali: The Beginning is an epic action-drama set in the fictional kingdom of Mahishmati. The story follows Shivudu, a young man with extraordinary strength and curiosity about his past. As he climbs a massive waterfall, he discovers the hidden truth about his royal lineage. He learns that he is the son of Amarendra Baahubali, a beloved warrior prince who was betrayed and killed. Shivudu, also known as Mahendra Baahubali, vows to avenge his father’s death and reclaim the throne from the tyrannical Bhallaladeva.
